Bacon

Description

This is a recipe for making cooked bacon, a very quick and simple way of making a meat snack, like sausages are.

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 rashers of back bacon

Steps

  1. Add your bacon to your cold frying pan.
  2. Heat the pan over medium heat for about 3 minutes.
  3. Turn the bacon over and heat for another 3 minutes or so.
  4. Take the bacon out of the pan and drain with a paper towel.
  5. Eat the bacon.
