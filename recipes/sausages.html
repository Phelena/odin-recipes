Sausages

Description

This is a recipe for making cooked sausages, a very quick and simple way of making a meat snack.

Ingredients

  • Three uncooked sausages (any kind)
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • Salt, pepper, or herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Add the oil to a frying pan.
  2. Heat the pan over medium heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the sausages to your pan.
  4. Every three minutes, turn the sausages over so all sides heat evenly.
  5. Sprinkle the sausages with salt, papper, or herbs, if wanted.
  6. After 12 minutes of cooking, take the sausages out of the pan.
  7. Let the sausages cool down for 2 minutes and eat them.
