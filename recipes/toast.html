Toast

Description

This is a recipe for making toast, otherwise known as cooked bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of brown bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster for 2 minutes.
  2. Keep toasting the bread if it's colour is too light for your liking.
  3. Take the toast out of the toaster when it's colour is to your liking.
  4. Spread light butter on the toast while it's warm.
  5. Eat the cooked bread.
